About us

RaiseYourVoice's MVP marks the beginning of a transformative journey to improve citizen
participation, government transparency, and accessibility to essential services in Nigeria. Our vision extends beyond the e-petition platform to include a comprehensive suite of tools and services that will revolutionize how citizens engage with their government. With your support, RaiseYourVoice will continue to evolve, enhancing the democratic process, empowering citizens, and contributing to a more transparent and efficient governance system in Nigeria. Investing in RaiseYourVoice means investing in the future of governance in Nigeria. Together, we can build a platform that truly empowers citizens, strengthens democracy, and enables secure and convenient virtual voting. Join us in realizing this vision and making a positive impact on Nigeria's governance landscape.

Objectives

RaiseYourVoice is a digital platform designed
to empower Nigerian citizensby providing a space for them to create,
sign, and promote petitions for changes in laws and government policies. The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of RaiseYourVoice aims to establish a functional e-petition platform, enabling citizens to participate actively in the democratic process and influence legislative decisions.

Our principle

Non-partisan RaiseYourVoice is neither left nor right, neither liberal nor conservative. We believe that all opinions deserve an equal hearing

who can host a petition

Anyone. Thanks to RaiseYourVoice, you don't have to be a big organization or a technical whiz to manage an online campaign. On the other hand, even if you are a large organization, you will still benefit from working with us. For larger organizations, we offer customized campaign-building solutions. These are total packages that can include anything from consulting services to web design and construction.

Its free

Yes. Our costs are covered by advertising and donations, so we are able to offer you these great tools totally free.
